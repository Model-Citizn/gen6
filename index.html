The Evolution

Six Generations of
Financial Modelling

Each generation absorbed the last. Gen 6 is the first to orchestrate the entire delivery chain — model, deck, and dashboard — from a single instruction.

1G
Cell-Based Formulas
Individual formulas in individual cells. VisiCalc, Lotus 1-2-3, early Excel.
1979 – 1990s
2G
Structured Standards
FAST, BPM, Operis, SMART. Separation of inputs, calcs, and outputs. Blue/black convention.
1990s – 2010s
3G
VBA & Macros
Automation via code. VBA macros handle repetitive tasks, data imports, and custom functions.
2000s – 2015
4G
Tables & Dynamic Arrays
Structured tables, Power Query, Power Pivot, dynamic arrays. Spill ranges change the game.
2015 – 2022
5G
Component-Based (LAMBDA)
Pre-built, pre-tested LAMBDA components. CBSE meets Excel.
2022 – present
Craig Hatmaker · Beyond Excel
6G
AI-Orchestrated
Human-built models, AI-delivered outputs. Agents generate the deck, publish the dashboard, orchestrate handoffs.
2026 →

How We Deliver

The 4D Process

Four phases. Clear boundaries. Our goal is to make you self-sufficient — not dependent on us.

Phase 1
Diagnose
Initial call to understand your current state, pain points, data sources, and what decisions the model needs to support.
Phase 2
Design
Scope the model architecture — components, drivers, scenarios, outputs. Agreed before a single formula is written.
Phase 3
Develop
Skilled modellers build the model to EXL Cloud standards. AI agents then generate the board deck and publish the interactive dashboard.
Phase 4
Deploy
Training, implementation, and handover. Your team runs the model. Ongoing support only if you need it.
Our intent is that you never need to call us. We build models with highly skilled humans, then hand them over so your team owns, understands, and operates them independently. No platform subscriptions. No consultant dependency. Just Excel, Claude, and your data. Xero-connected models include a small ongoing subscription for live data integration.
