Gen 2 Workbook Upgrade

Every EXL Cloud workbook has been rebuilt to the Gen 2 standard — a cleaner, more consistent experience across every file you work with.

  • Instructions Sheet

    Every workbook now includes a dedicated Instructions sheet with clear guidance on how to use the file, what the formulas do, and how to get the most out of it with Claude's skills.

  • Improved Navigation

    Cleaner structure so you spend less time finding what you need and more time on the analysis that matters.

  • Consistent Formatting

    Uniform layout across all workbooks — moving between files feels seamless and familiar.

  • Formula Transparency

    Comprehensive plain-English notes on complex formulas. No more black magic numbers — see exactly where every calculation comes from.
